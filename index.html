MISSION

"In partnership with our parents, staff, and community, Crockett ISD will provide quality educational experiences for students to succeed in a competitive, diverse, and ever-changing society."

2018 - 2019 DISTRICT GOALS

Crockett ISD will : 1. Challenge and encourage all students to excel academically; 2. Provide resources and services to assure the success of all special population groups; 3. Plan for recruitment, retention, and training of highly qualified administrators, teachers, paraprofessionals and other staff; 4. Maintain a safe and drug free environment ; 5. Provide opportunities for parent and community involvement. Crockett I.S.D. is proud of its staff, students, parents and community. We at Crockett I.S.D. consider ourselves blessed to educate and support the children in Crockett, Texas. Everyone at Crockett I.S.D. welcomes you to our online website. Our goal is to provide you with the information you need when you need it. If for some reason you do not find what you are looking for, please contact the appropriate staff member. We are here to serve you. Crockett I.S.D. is very excited to offer many new online features for staff, students, parents and the community. Crockett I.S.D. encourages parents to work along side the staff to insure the best for our students. You will find a new parent portal that gives you access to your child's educational opportunities and activities.
